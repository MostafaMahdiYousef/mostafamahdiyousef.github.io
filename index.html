Welcome to my personal website! I am Mostafa Mahdi Yousef, an Electrical Engineer and Ph.D. researcher at York University, specializing in the cutting-edge development of EV on-board chargers with V2X capability and advanced State of Charge (SoC) estimation techniques. With a foundation of excellence from Sharif University of Technology—where I designed dynamic state estimation methods for power systems—and a B.Sc. in optimizing solar energy through MPPT algorithms, I blend innovation with technical expertise. My professional journey includes immersive industry experience at one of the largest power plants in the Middle East. From renewable energy systems to smart grids, I am passionate about driving sustainable advancements in power electronics and control systems. Let’s innovate the future of clean energy together!
